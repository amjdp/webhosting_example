About Us

Nrityanjali School for Classical Dance

For a decade,Nrityanjali has been grooming dancers of the future and training them to be at ease
with their body and soul,tradition and modernity, past and present. The structure of school is authentically
traditional and ecofriendly. The courses offered are :
  • Bharatanatyam
  • Mohiniyattam
  • Kuchupudi
  • Keralanadanam
We generally advocate that a child be at least 5 years old before beginning lessons in dance and music.
Professional dancers and dance teachers are given training in specialized areas like Choreography,
Nattuvangam, Thalam structure and Natya Sastra. This considerably simplifies the communication
between the teacher and the taught.
